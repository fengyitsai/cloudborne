The purest idea of flight

Drifting above an endless sea of clouds.

No scores. No objectives. Just the wind, the clouds, and the horizon beyond.

Cloudborne is the purest idea of flight. You don't have to do anything — just look up and watch the clouds drift by.

Let the sky carry the moment

Tilt your phone, and the clouds part for you. Set it down, and the world drifts on without you.

Climb higher, and the cloud sea stretches below like cotton. Sink into the clouds, and white mist wraps gently around you.

Sunlight slips through the gaps, and the sky shifts color with every change in altitude. No two moments are the same.

Perfect for

  • The end of a long day, when you want to think about nothing
  • Sleepless nights — let the clouds carry you to dreams
  • When you need a quiet corner, just for a moment
  • Or simply when you want to look at the sky
