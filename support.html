Getting Started

Cloudborne is a serene flight experience. Simply launch the app and you'll be floating above the clouds. Tilt your device to steer, or enable Cruise mode for auto-pilot.

Controls

Tilt — Tilt your device to control flight direction (on physical devices).

Touch — Pan on screen to steer (on simulator or as fallback).

Single Tap — Reveals the settings gear icon.

Debug Panel

Tap the gear icon to open the debug panel. Here you can adjust cloud size, density, coverage, camera altitude, and cruise parameters. All settings are saved automatically.

Cruise Mode

Enable Cruise in the debug panel for auto-pilot. Adjust speed, turn intensity, pitch, and altitude range to customize your flight path.

Attitude Indicator

Toggle the attitude indicator in the debug panel to see a classic artificial horizon showing your pitch and roll angles.
